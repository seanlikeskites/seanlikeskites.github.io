#### Chongqing Xiaomian based on the recipe in Fuchia Dunlop's The Food of Sichuan.

Made using hand pulled lamian to get the practice hours in. My lamian skills are getting more consistent.

Topped with:
  * Fried Pork Mince
  * Crispy Chickpeas
  * Soy Bean Sprouts
  * Blanched Lettuce
